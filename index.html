Contest Guidelines

Essential rules and requirements for participation

Article Requirements

  • Minimum 3,500 bytes for new articles
  • Must follow Wikipedia's NPOV, Verifiability, and No Original Research policies
  • No plagiarism or copyright violations
  • Proper sourcing and references required

Eligible Content

  • Religious practices and rituals
  • Cultural traditions and customs
  • Notable Islamic figures and scholars
  • Ramadan-inspired arts and literature

Participation Rules

  • Open to all registered Wikipedia users
  • Team contributions allowed with shared prizes
  • Multiple submissions per participant welcomed
  • All contributions must meet quality standards
